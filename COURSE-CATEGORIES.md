## Cyber Security

## Blockchain

## Quantum Computing

### Notes

- The two "AI Security" courses (**AI Security Analyst**, **AI Security and Governance Management**)
  sit on the AI/Cyber Security boundary. They are placed under **Artificial Intelligence** because
  their primary domain is securing and governing AI systems themselves. On the live site,
  *AI Security Analyst* is tagged "AI + Security".
- 11 courses total: 5 Artificial Intelligence, 3 Cyber Security, 2 Blockchain, 1 Quantum Computing.
